# Privacy Policy for ProteinChef

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

## Overview

ProteinChef ("we", "our", "us") is a recipe app that helps users find high-protein meals. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information We Collect

ProteinChef does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely on your device.

- We do **not** require account creation or login
- We do **not** collect your name, email, or phone number
- We do **not** access your contacts, camera, or location
- We do **not** track your activity across other apps or websites

## Data Storage

All your preferences (such as saved recipes or favorites) are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

## Third-Party Services

ProteinChef may use third-party APIs (such as Spoonacular) to provide recipe data and images. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not send any personal information to these services.

## Analytics

We do **not** use Google Analytics or any other analytics or tracking tools.

## Advertising

ProteinChef does **not** display ads.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
