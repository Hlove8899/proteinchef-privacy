# Privacy Policy for ProteinChef

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

## Overview

ProteinChef ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered recipe app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information We Collect

ProteinChef collects minimal information to provide a personalized experience:

- **Name:** We ask for your first name during onboarding to personalize the app. This is stored locally on your device only.
- **Email address:** If you choose to sign in with Apple, we may receive your Apple ID email address for account identification.
- **Camera:** We access your camera only when you choose to scan ingredients. Photos are processed on-device and are not uploaded or stored.
- **User ID:** A unique identifier is generated for account management and to sync your subscription status.
- **Purchase History:** We collect purchase and subscription data through RevenueCat to manage your Pro membership.
- **Product Interaction:** We collect data on how you interact with the app (e.g., recipes viewed, features used) to improve the experience.
- **Crash Data:** We collect crash logs and diagnostics to identify and fix bugs.

We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

## Data Storage

Your health goals and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

## Third-Party Services

- **OpenAI** — processes ingredient photos to generate recipes
- **RevenueCat** — manages subscription purchases
- **Apple Sign In** — handles authentication

## Advertising

ProteinChef does not display ads.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
